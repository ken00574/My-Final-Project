Transcripts

Please Note: Electronic transcripts are currently available. We have a limited ability to process paper transcripts. Rush service will not be available. Please do not fax or mail your request.

A transcript is a complete and faithful copy of the student's University academic record. Official transcripts bear the seal of the University and the signature of the registrar.

Delivery Options

Electronic: Delivered through the National Student Clearinghouse (Not available for pre-1984 transcripts). Mailed: Sent to the requested address through USPS first class mail. - Limited availability Pick-up: Photo ID is required. - Currently not available Please note: We do not fax transcripts.
